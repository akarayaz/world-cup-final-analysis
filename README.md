# World Cup Final Analysis (Argentina vs France)

This project presents a data-driven analysis of the **2022 FIFA World Cup Final** between Argentina and France using event-level football data.

The objective is to combine statistical analysis with tactical interpretation to better understand how the match unfolded, highlighting both team structures and individual brilliance.

---

## 📊 Data Source

The data used in this project is obtained from **StatsBomb open event data**.

It includes detailed, event-level information such as:

* Passes (start and end locations)
* Shots and expected goals (xG)
* Defensive actions
* Player involvement across the pitch

---

## ⚙️ Tools & Libraries

* Python
* pandas
* matplotlib
* mplsoccer

---

## 🔍 Analysis Overview

The notebook explores the match through multiple analytical lenses:

### 1. Lionel Messi – Passing Map & Influence

* Visualizes Messi’s passing behavior using heatmaps and directional arrows
* Highlights how he operated across different zones of the pitch
* Shows his role as a central playmaker in Argentina’s attacking structure

---

### 2. Kylian Mbappé – Shooting & Carrying Threat

* Focuses on Mbappé’s offensive contribution
* Shot maps and carries illustrate his explosive impact
* Captures how he changed the game in short, decisive moments

---

### 3. Argentina Midfield – Defensive Activity

* Visualizes pressures, interceptions, duels, and recoveries
* Demonstrates Argentina’s midfield intensity and control
* Highlights their ability to disrupt France’s build-up play

---

### 4. Team Heatmaps 

* Visualizes overall team activity across the pitch using heatmaps  
* Highlights territorial dominance and preferred zones of play  
* Argentina’s heatmap shows strong central and attacking-third presence, reflecting sustained pressure and structured build-up  
* France’s activity is more limited and fragmented for much of the match, increasing only in later stages  

This comparison provides a clear view of how each team occupied space and controlled different areas of the pitch throughout the game.

---

### 5. Team Passing Networks 

* Constructs a passing network based on completed passes
* Node size represents player involvement (pass volume)
* Edge thickness reflects connection strength between players

This analysis reveals:

* Team structure and spacing
* Key hubs in ball circulation
* Overall tactical organization

---

### 6. Cumulative xG Analysis

* Tracks expected goals (xG) over time using step plots
* Compares Argentina and France’s chance creation throughout the match
  
---

## 🧠 Key Takeaways

* Argentina demonstrates structured dominance and sustained chance creation
* France struggles to generate opportunities within organized play
* The match is ultimately shaped by moments of individual brilliance, particularly from Mbappé

---

## 📌 Conclusion

This analysis illustrates how football matches can be understood through both:

* **systematic team performance**, and
* **high-impact individual actions**

While Argentina controlled the game statistically, France remained competitive through efficiency and decisive moments.
